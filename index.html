KiKi’s Journey

^^

Kiki is a young witch from Studio Ghibli’s classic film Kiki’s Delivery Service. At just thirteen years old, she leaves home with her talking cat Jiji to find her place in the world. With her broomstick, she starts a small delivery service in a seaside town, learning about independence, friendship, and self-confidence along the way. Kiki’s story inspires us to believe in ourselves, even when times get tough.
